My Favorite Type of Cookie.

My favorite type of cookie is Chips Ahoy Chewy.
I love the soft texture and the rich chocolate chips that melt in your mouth.
It's the perfect treat for any time of day.
Whether I've had a long day or just need a little pick-me-up,
Chips Ahoy Chewy always hits the spot.
Chocolate chip cookies are a classic favorite,
and Chips Ahoy Chewy takes it to the next level with its soft and chewy texture.
They're my guilty pleasure and I can't resist having a few whenever I see them.
And just recently, I discovered the Big Chips Ahoy,
it's like the regular Chips Ahoy but bigger and even more delicious!
